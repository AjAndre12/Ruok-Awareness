Real Stories from Real People

Here are some heartwarming stories from people who took the step to ask someone if they were OK, and how that simple question made a difference:

Emily's Story

Emily noticed her friend Sarah was becoming increasingly withdrawn. One day, she asked, "Are you OK?" This simple question opened the door to a deep conversation, and Emily was able to support Sarah through a tough time in her life.

Tom's Story

Tom was going through a rough patch and felt isolated. A colleague at work noticed the change in his behavior and reached out with concern. That conversation was the beginning of Tom’s recovery and his renewed sense of hope.

Jasmine's Story

Jasmine was struggling with balancing her studies and personal life. When her friend asked if she was OK, Jasmine felt a weight lift off her shoulders. She realized she wasn’t alone and sought the help she needed.
